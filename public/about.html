About Me

Hello, my name is Angie Augustin, and I am a passionate software developer currently working on
building my career in the tech industry. I was born in Haiti and moved to the United States at
the age of 7, which provided me with a unique perspective and a deep appreciation for hard work,
resilience, and the opportunities available in the tech world

I began my journey in software development with a focus on front-end development, where I
discovered my love for creating user-friendly, functional, and visually appealing web applications.
As I continue to grow in my skills, I’ve expanded my expertise into full-stack development, allowing me to
build both the client and server-side of applications. This progression has deepened my understanding
of how to create seamless and efficient solutions from start to finish.

I am driven by my desire to solve problems, build impactful solutions, and continuously learn
new technologies. My journey as a first-generation college graduate has instilled in me a strong work ethic
and perseverance, values that I carry with me as I grow in this dynamic field. I’m excited about the
opportunities that lie ahead and am committed to contributing to the tech industry with both creativity
and technical expertise.
